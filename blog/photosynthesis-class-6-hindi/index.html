Photosynthesis — Class 6 Simple Explainer (Hinglish)

3 April 2026 · Science · NCERT-style basics

Photosynthesis ko Hindi mein often prakash sanshleshan (प्रकाश संश्लेषण) bolte hain. Simple meaning: paudhe sunlight use karke apna khana banate hain — aur is process mein oxygen release hota hai jo hum saans lete hain.

Kya chahiye? (Raw materials)

  • Carbon dioxide (CO₂) — hawa se leaves ke chhote pores (stomata) se andar.
  • Water — roots se plant ke andar transport.
  • Sunlight — energy source.

Kahan hota hai?

Green leaves mein chlorophyll pigment hota hai jo sunlight absorb karta hai. Isi wajah se most plants green dikhte hain.

Output kya nikalta hai?

  • Glucose / starch — plant ka food (energy store).
  • Oxygen — by-product jo atmosphere mein chhod dete hain.

Exam one-liner style: Carbon dioxide + water, in presence of sunlight and chlorophyll, gives glucose and oxygen.

Kyun important hai?

Photosynthesis food chain ki starting hai. Bina iske plants grow nahi karte, phir herbivores, phir ecosystem — sab disturb.

Quick recall (Class 6)

Sunlight + chlorophyll + CO₂ + water → food for plant + O₂ for us. Agar yeh flow khud se bina dekhe bol paaye, samjho concept almost clear.
