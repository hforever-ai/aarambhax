SAAVI vs Tuition: What Hindi Medium Students Should Know

3 April 2026 · EdTech · Hinglish

Tuition safe default lagta hai — lekin har family ke liye affordable ya comfortable nahi. SAAVI, Shrutam app ki AI teacher didi, isliye design hui hai taaki Hindi / Hinglish students ko pace aur language dono mile, bina class mein “dumb” feel kiye.

Seedhi baat: SAAVI school ya achhe teachers ki jagah nahi le sakti jahan lab, discipline, aur peer learning matter karta hai. Yeh tool revision + doubt layer hai.

1. Language comfort

Kai tuition centres abhi bhi English-only explain karte hain — samajh aata hai, lekin slow. SAAVI Hinglish + Hindi mix mein repeat, simplify, analogy de sakti hai jab tak concept clear na ho.

2. Cost aur time

Monthly tuition + travel often financial burden ban jata hai. Shrutam ki pricing FAQ mein outline hai (intro offers possible). Time side: raat 11 baje doubt — tuition band; app layer available rehti hai jab net available ho.

3. Judgment & shame

Class mein galat jawab bolne ka dar real hai. Voice-first AI flow is context mein low-pressure practice deta hai — galati = redo, insult nahi.

4. Kab tuition better rahega

  • Agar tumhe strict handwritten notes aur face-to-face accountability chahiye.
  • Agar board / school ne very local paper pattern diya ho aur wahi master karna ho — phir local teacher gold hai.

Ideal mix: school + self revision on Shrutam, aur selective doubt classes jahan weak ho.
