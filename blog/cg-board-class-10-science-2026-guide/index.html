CG Board Class 10 Science 2026: A Practical Prep Guide

3 April 2026 · Chhattisgarh Board · Class 10 Science

Class 10 board year pressure real hai — especially Science jahan theory, diagrams, aur practicals teeno ek saath maangte hain. Yeh guide CG Board (Chhattisgarh) mindset se likha hai, lekin NCERT-style concepts CBSE students ke liye bhi useful hain.

1. Pehle syllabus ko “weight” do

Har chapter ko equal time mat do. Heavy chapters (jaise light, electricity, carbon compounds, life processes) ko pehle identify karo. Ek simple rule: jis chapter mein diagram + numerical + definition teen mix hain, wahan revision double.

School teacher ka sequence follow karo, lekin apna short list banao — “weak trio” jo tumhare mock tests mein baar baar galti dete hain.

2. Practical file aur viva

CG Board practicals mein procedure, observation, inference clean likhna marks bachata hai. Viva ke liye har practical ka one-line purpose yaad rakho — examiner often wahi poochte hain.

  • Diagrams neat — axis labels mat bhoolna.
  • Units har numerical mein — careless mistakes yahi se aate hain.

3. Theory revision: active recall

Sirf padhna kam hai — khud se bolna zyada kaam karta hai. Har topic ke liye 60-second “explain like didi” try karo. Agar atak jao, note karo: yahi gap hai.

Diagrams

Eye, human heart, AC/DC circuits — inko blank paper pe 3 baar bina dekhe banao. Third attempt usually exam-ready hota hai.

4. Previous year papers ka sahi use

Past papers pattern dikhate hain, answer bank nahi. Har paper ke baad sirf yeh count karo: kaunsa chapter zyada poochha gaya, kaunsi trap language repeat hui. Wahi chapters next week ke plan mein top pe.
